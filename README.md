## About Nexora Admin
**Nexora Admin** is a comprehensive Open Source ERP and CRM solution designed for small to medium enterprises. Manage your entire business workflow—from initial quotes to final payments—in one unified dashboard.

Built on the advanced **MERN Stack** (Node.js, Express, MongoDB, React) and styled with **Ant Design (AntD)** and **Redux**.

## 🚀 Key Features
- **Invoice & Quote Management:** Create professional documents in seconds.
- **Payment Tracking:** Record and monitor all incoming revenue.
- **Customer CRM:** Manage your client relationships and history.
- **Inventory & Accounting:** Basic modules to track your business health.
- **Modern UI:** Responsive design powered by Ant Design.
